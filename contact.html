Frequently Asked Questions

How can I volunteer with Hope Foundation?

You can volunteer by filling out our volunteer application form on this page or by emailing volunteer@hopefoundation.org. We have various opportunities both locally and internationally.

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes, Hope Foundation is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization, and all donations are tax-deductible to the extent allowed by law.

How can my company partner with Hope Foundation?

We offer various corporate partnership opportunities, including sponsorships, cause marketing, and employee engagement programs. Please contact us at partnerships@hopefoundation.org to discuss possibilities.
